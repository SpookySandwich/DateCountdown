# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 26, 2026

Date Countdowns ("the Application") is a desktop app designed to help users track countdowns to important dates. This Privacy Policy describes how the Application handles your data.

## Data Collection and Usage

**Date Countdowns does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

- **Local Processing:** Countdown titles, dates, notification preferences, widget data, and app settings are stored and processed locally on your device.
- **No Telemetry:** The Application does not include analytics, tracking, telemetry, or advertising mechanisms.
- **No Account Required:** You do not need to create an account or provide any personal information to use the Application.
- **No External Servers:** The Application does not send your countdown data to any external server.

## Notifications, Tiles, and Widgets

The Application may use Windows features such as notifications, Start menu tiles, startup tasks, and widgets when you enable them. These features use locally stored countdown data to display reminders or countdown information on your device.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
